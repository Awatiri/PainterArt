Creating Timeless Memories

For over two decades, I've been passionate about capturing the essence of life through portraiture and fine art. Based in the beautiful Oakland Hills, my studio is where stories come to life on canvas.

Each painting is a collaboration between artist and subject, resulting in artwork that doesn't just decorate your walls—it becomes part of your family's legacy. From beloved pets to cherished family moments, I specialize in creating pieces that resonate with emotional depth and artistic excellence.

My work has been featured in local galleries and private collections throughout the Bay Area, but my greatest joy comes from seeing families connect with their commissioned pieces for generations to come.
